The Math used

Disclaimer

The math that I used is based of the amazing works of Nicolas Gillis, Prem Melville, Vikas Sindhwani and Albert Au Yeung.
All I did was take some ideas from their papers and mix and match techniques untill I figured what gives the best results with my dataset(cleaned version).

About the dataset

As you can see from the links above, the dataset is very sparse. I got it from students from the University of Campinas using a very simple form from Google Forms. The results probably would`ve been a lot better if I used a bigger and nicer dataset, but those aren't very easy to come by, especially in the format that I expected.

Design choices

When $a \ne 0$, there are two solutions to \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\) and they are $$x = {-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac} \over 2a}.$$
